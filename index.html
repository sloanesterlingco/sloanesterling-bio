What MyHealthVaultAI does

Core functions

MyHealthVaultAI is a standalone health app patients can download themselves. It’s built to return information back to the person living the life — while aligning with the EMR environments most providers already use.

01 · Health vault
All your history in one place
Medications, allergies, surgeries, and conditions stored in a shareable, structured format.
02 · Safety intelligence
Medication checks & alerts
Track active meds and surface possible contraindications to review with your provider.
03 · Real-time data
Bluetooth health monitoring
Connect compatible devices for vitals and trends that travel with you into each visit.
04 · Document capture
OCR for labs & imaging
Scan lab results, imaging reports, and office summaries directly into your vault.
05 · Visit workflow
Structured visit prep
Walk into check-in with a clean summary so less time is spent re-answering the basics.
06 · EMR awareness
Built for integration
Designed to map into the systems clinics already use instead of fighting against them.

Founder story

Why this exists

I’m Sloane Sterling — a practicing PA-C and systems-minded builder focused on how health, technology, and real life intersect. MyHealthVaultAI came from seeing how much of healthcare gets lost between what patients remember, what providers have time to ask, and what actually lands in the chart.

The app is designed to give patients control over their own health story while respecting the realities of a busy clinic — clearing space so real care can happen with more clarity and less friction on both sides of the exam room door.
