About

Context file

I’m Sloane Sterling — a forward-leaning creator and founder operating where fitness, technology, and global movement intersect. My world is built around systems: how you train, how you travel, how you work, and how the tools around you either accelerate or slow all of that down.

The Sloane ecosystem now expands beyond content alone. I’m in active production on a premium women’s activewear line and in development on medical applications designed for public-domain use — tools meant to integrate with the electronic medical record systems most providers already live in every day.

Brand ecosystem

Parallel builds
In production • Apparel
LuxeSculpt™ – Women's Activewear Line
Status: Production process underway · Target: Summer 2026 launch

A premium activewear line for women that treats every piece like performance equipment — engineered for training, travel, and real-world movement. Think material innovation, biomechanical fit, and durability that holds up from airport gates to heavy sets.

The goal is simple: create gear that feels as considered as the systems behind it — pieces that belong in a carry-on and on a competition floor, not just in passive athleisure shots.

In development • Medical applications
Sloane Sterling Medical Systems
Focus: Public-domain tools · EMR integration · Workflow clarity

A set of medical applications built for public-domain use, designed to plug into the electronic medical record platforms most providers already use. The intent: reduce friction across patient workflows, documentation, and follow-up — without asking clinicians to learn yet another standalone system.

Compatibility and interoperability are core design constraints. These tools are being shaped to align with common EMR environments so that better experiences for patients and providers can be deployed where care already happens.
