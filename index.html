About

Context file

I’m Sloane Sterling — a future-facing creator and founder operating where fitness, technology, and global movement intersect. My work is built around systems: how you train, how you travel, how you work, and how the tools around you either accelerate or slow all of that down.

The Sloane ecosystem expands beyond content alone. I’m in the production process of a premium activewear line of women’s clothing and in development on medical applications designed for public-domain use — tools meant to integrate with the common electronic medical record software most providers already use every day.

In production · Apparel
LuxeSculpt™ – Women’s Activewear Line
Status: Production process underway · Target launch: Summer 2026

A premium activewear line for women that treats every piece like performance equipment — engineered for training, travel, and real-world movement. Think material innovation, biomechanical fit, and durability that holds up from airport gates to heavy sets.

The intent is simple: pieces that belong in a carry-on and on a competition floor, not just in passive athleisure shots.

In development · Medical applications
Sloane Sterling Medical Systems
Focus: Public-domain tools · EMR integration · Workflow clarity

A set of medical applications built for public-domain use and designed to plug into the electronic medical record platforms most providers already use. The goal is to reduce friction across patient workflows, documentation, and follow-up — without asking clinicians to learn yet another standalone system.

Compatibility and interoperability are non-negotiable. These tools are being shaped to align with common EMR environments so better experiences for patients and providers can be deployed where care already happens.
